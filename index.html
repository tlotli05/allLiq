FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Can kids attend both day and night events?

Kids are welcome for the daytime family event (9AM-3PM) which is free for children under 12. The evening festival (6PM-till we drop) is strictly 18+ only. Kids must be accompanied by a parent or guardian at all times during the day event.

What are the swimming pool rules?

Swimming pool opens at 9am. Daytime (9AM-5PM) is family-friendly with shallow kids area and lifeguards. Evening (6PM-12AM) is 18+ pool party. Bring your own towels. No glass containers near the pool. Lifeguards on duty at all times.

Is there parking and transportation?

Yes! Secure parking available on-site for R50 per vehicle. VIP parking R150 (limited spaces). Shuttle buses run from Nelspruit (R80 return) and Middelburg (R100 return). Book shuttles in advance via our website.

What food and drinks are available?

Daytime: Family food court with burgers, pizza, ice cream, and kid-friendly options. Evening: Gourmet food trucks, vegetarian/vean options, cocktail bars, craft beer garden. No outside food or drinks allowed.

Are tickets refundable?

Tickets are non-refundable but transferable. You can transfer your ticket to someone else up to 48 hours before the event through Computicket. Event happens rain or shine.

What safety measures are in place?

Medical tents with trained staff, security personnel throughout venue, well-lit areas, free water stations, designated safe zones, visible security and medical contact points.

What if it rains on the day?

The festival happens rain or shine! We have covered areas for both events. Check our social media for weather updates and bring appropriate clothing. No refunds due to weather.
